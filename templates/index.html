FAQ

How do I issue a certificate on the platform?

Connect your Nami Wallet, enter the certificate details and sign the transaction. Once the hash is submitted, a transaction ID will confirm successful issuance.

What should I do if my transaction doesnâ€™t immediately go through?

Wait at least 20 seconds for the blockchain to process the block, then refresh and resubmit the transaction if needed.

Can I verify a certificate without a Nami Wallet?

No, you would need to connect a Nami wallet to be able to use the prototype. As the project progresses, other Cardano wallets would be accepted.

How can I get test ADA into my wallet to test the prototype?

You can obtain test ADA by connecting your wallet to a Cardano testnet faucet. Search for "Cardano testnet faucet" and follow the instructions.

What happens if I lose the certificate hash after issuance?

The hash is essential for verification. Without it, the certificate cannot be verified, so make sure to copy and securely store it after issuance.
